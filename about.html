About Us

Who We Are

Namma Masala is a spice business company operating from September 2020 as a sole ole Proprietor ownership company run by a woman entrepreneur. Namma Masala currently provides retail packing and repacking over 4 varieties of whole spice products such as Pepper, Fenugreek, Mustard and Cumin. Based on our quality product recognition in the market and customer demands, we have extended our business to supply lentils too. Our goal is to generate employment opportunities to agricultural labourers and rural workers. The company employs mostly woman from the rural areas to let them lead an honorary life.

What Our Mission Is

Our mission is to become one of the spice companies that sells high quality spices, nuts and lentils at affordable prices.

What Our Vision Is

Our vision is to become one of the most valued business partners, and to create more employment opportunities, especially for women.

What we offer

Namma Masala offers a range of whole spices, lentils and nuts that are perfect for homecooks, as well as for professional chefs. Our products enhance and completely rejuvenate cooking with freshness. Our whole spice packaging sizes range from 50 grams to 1000 grams.
